🏺 How to Decipher the Script

1. Look at the glyph sequence - These 4 symbols form a meaningful sentence.
2. Read the clues IN ORDER - Clue 1 tells you something. Clue 2 builds on that. Clue 3 narrows it further. Each clue has a green conclusion.
Example deduction chain:
Clue 1: "Symbol A appears on royal objects" → Symbol A = KING
Clue 2: "The symbol after KING means a relationship" → Second symbol = SON
Clue 3: "The last symbol appears in tombs" → Last symbol = DIED or REST
Clue 4: "The sentence means: The king's son died" → Third symbol = DIED, Fourth = REST
3. Assign meanings - Click buttons in the matrix. Green highlighted buttons are recommended by the clues.
4. Type the full sentence - When all 4 glyphs have meanings, type the complete English sentence.
