🏺 How to Decipher Ancient Script

1️⃣ Look at the glyphs — These are the mysterious symbols carved into the temple wall.
2️⃣ Read the clues — Archaeologists found fragments that give hints. Some say "Glyph X appears near royal objects" or "Glyph Y is NOT meaning Z."
3️⃣ Assign meanings — Click the buttons to match each glyph to a word. The CSP checker will warn you if you create a logical conflict.
4️⃣ Translate the phrase — Once all glyphs have meanings, type how you think the whole phrase reads in order.
🎯 Goal — Find the CORRECT hidden meanings. Your assignments must be logically consistent AND match the ancient scribe's intent.
