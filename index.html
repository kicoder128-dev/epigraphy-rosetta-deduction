🏺 How Logical Deduction Works

1. The Inscription
You see a sequence of glyphs. Your job: find what each glyph means and what the whole phrase says.
2. The Clues Are Connected
Unlike random hints, each clue builds on others. For example:
Clue A: "The glyph before ⚰️ means a family role"
Clue B: "⚰️ appears in burial contexts"
Deduction: ⚰️ might mean DIED or REST
Clue C: "The family role glyph is never with royalty"
Deduction: Family role ≠ KING, so it's SON → ⚰️ is DIED (since REST goes with royalty)
3. The Deduction Panel
Tracks what you can logically conclude. Use it to guide your assignments.
4. The Matrix
Assign meanings. The game will cross out impossible options based on your deductions.
